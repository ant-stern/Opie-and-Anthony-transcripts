The Opie and Anthony show began with a report on their listener count, revealing a net loss of 20 listeners following the previous day’s broadcast, which featured a controversial segment on conjoined twins.  The hosts then read several angry emails from listeners outraged by this and other recent segments, including one detailing a personal tragedy involving anencephalic infant.  This segued into a discussion of the Drudge Report's breaking news about Bill Clinton and Monica Lewinsky and a cigar.  The conversation abruptly shifted to a segment from the Guinness Book of World Records TV show showcasing a man with removable facial prosthetics.  Opie and Anthony, along with callers, expressed strong disgust and revulsion at the man’s appearance, using terms like "sick" and "disgusting."  The segment generated a flood of calls and emails echoing their visceral reactions.  Listeners also called in to discuss the hosts' contest to have a woman expose her breasts on the Today Show, with one caller recounting a missed opportunity to photograph a woman who had written the show's name on her breasts.  Hurricane Bonnie was briefly discussed with the hosts' characteristic sarcasm.  Interspersed throughout were listener-requested songs.  A plastic surgeon phoned in to explain the process of creating and applying the type of facial prosthetics featured on the Guinness World Records segment.  The show concluded with continued discussion of the prosthetic man, more listener calls, and the usual Opie and Anthony banter.
