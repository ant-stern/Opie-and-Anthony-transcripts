SHOW SUMMARY:

The Opie and Anthony pre-show, hosted by Sam Roberts, started with a recap of Thursday's broadcast, which notably featured a studio visit from Lawrence Fishburne.  Clips played highlighted Fishburne's humorous anecdotes about his experiences with Marlon Brando and Harvey Keitel, including a near-exploding boat engine incident during the filming of *Apocalypse Now*, and a comical recounting of his *Pee-wee's Playhouse* audition where he initially misconstrued the role.  The pre-show then shifted to a discussion of Thursday's contentious on-air gun control debate, which involved Jim Norton sparring with callers.  The ongoing Twitter feud between Anthony Cumia and a user, “Agatha” (suspected to be a white male troll), was also mentioned.  Other topics covered included the L.A. Kings Stanley Cup victory (with commentary on Roland's fluctuating team loyalties) and a viral video of a woman's spectacular post-game ice fall.  Chris Christie's "Evolution of Dad Dance" on the *Tonight Show* was also discussed.  The death of Casey Kasem led to a phone call with Dr. Steve regarding feeding tubes.  Anthony Weiner's latest Twitter mishap – accidentally favoriting a sexting article – was another subject. The impact of readily available internet pornography on the strip club industry was explored, with a DJ from a Canadian strip club calling in to detail the increasingly elaborate and theatrical strip shows being implemented as a countermeasure. The pre-show concluded abruptly before the main show's start.
