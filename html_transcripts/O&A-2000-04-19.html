SHOW SUMMARY:

The Opie and Anthony show featured Filter's Richard Patrick (lead vocals) and Gino (guitar) as guests. The interview began with discussion of Filter's upcoming Hammerstein Ballroom show and their song "Hey Man, Nice Shot," inspired by the suicide of Bud Dwyer.  This segued into a graphic discussion of online videos, including footage of Dwyer's suicide and a woman being hit by a train.  The conversation then devolved into discussions of pornography, with anecdotes about finding porn in hotel rooms and discovering evidence of sexual activity in their own office.  Patrick and Gino recounted their experiences with excessive drinking and debauchery during tour flights, with Patrick detailing the inspiration behind "Take a Picture," relating it to a drunken airplane incident.  He also discussed his relationship with his father and his father's fight against prostate cancer. Opie and Anthony interjected with criticisms of various pop artists and lamented the financial struggles many musicians face due to mismanagement.  The discussion also included a segment on Filter's guitars, Patrick's departure from Nine Inch Nails (attributed to "boredom"), and a caller's positive feedback on a remix of "Take a Picture."
