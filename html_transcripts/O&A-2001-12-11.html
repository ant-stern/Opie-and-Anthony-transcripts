SHOW SUMMARY:

The Opie and Anthony Show broadcast detailed "Homeless Shopping Spree 2," a chaotic event involving ten homeless individuals – Sperm, Tippi Tom, Rock Your Old Tommy, Blue, Buttered Roll, Snoop, Ziggy, Big Ed, Big Blue, and Andre – each given $100, food, beer, socks, and pornography.  The journey to Roosevelt Field Mall on Long Island, on a bus equipped with video screens and a bathroom, was fraught with escalating debauchery.  Numerous on-site reports from show staff (Ben, Keith, Psycho Mark, Rick), guests Jim Norton and Rich Voss, and listeners painted a picture of unrestrained intoxication, violence, and bizarre behavior.  Tippi Tom, initially asleep, became aggressive upon waking, threatening violence and arguing over money. Rock Your Old Tommy attempted to light himself on fire. Blue engaged in sexually aggressive behavior towards a female staff member, Mary. Ziggy believed he was on an airplane.

The hosts, Opie and Anthony, in the studio, engaged in numerous phone calls with those on the bus, listeners, and mall security, debating the wisdom of providing beer (with Keith expressing concerns).  At least one beer was given to Rock Your Old Tommy, resulting in increased pandemonium.  Listeners reported a large caravan of vehicles following the bus and an increased police presence near the mall, with conflicting reports regarding the actual number of police and security personnel.  Sergeant Klain's concern over open containers led to a frantic attempt to hide empty bottles.

Upon arrival at the mall, the scene was described as utter chaos, with hundreds of people, many with cameras, creating a massive crowd chanting "Let the Homeless Shop!"  The event involved significant crowd participation.  The shopping spree itself was marked by various humorous and disruptive incidents involving the homeless participants and store staff.  Tommy jumped into a tub display at a bar. Some stores refused entry to the group. One individual attempted to leave without paying.  Despite challenges, the homeless individuals obtained clothing, electronics, and food.  Psycho Mark's drunken antics, including yelling, potentially losing his teeth, and attempting to rub his genitals against a CD collection, were a recurring theme.  Big Blue enjoyed a massage chair, and Ziggy celebrated a nice restroom.  Dippy Tom engaged in impromptu Christmas carol singing. One caller criticized the show as unfunny and unoriginal, while others offered both support and criticism.  The broadcast ended abruptly with the participants boarding the bus for the return to New York City, with three individuals unaccounted for.  A final caller reported at least 2,000 people at the mall exit cheering.  The hosts promised to share photos and videos online and continue the story the next day.  Privileged Night Club was a sponsor but their promised strippers did not appear.  Snoop, a former homeless individual helped by the show, called in to express gratitude.  The entire broadcast was punctuated by crude and offensive language.
