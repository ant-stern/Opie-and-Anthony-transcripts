Callers added jokes and reminisced about past show antics.  The segment concluded with preparations for the challenge, EMTs on standby, and contestants filling out extensive paperwork, alongside tangential conversations about the hosts' job security and an upcoming holiday show.  Numerous off-color jokes and sexually suggestive remarks were made throughout.
