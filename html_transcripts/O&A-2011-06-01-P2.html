SHOW SUMMARY:

The "After Opie & Anthony Live" broadcast with Sam Roberts began with playful ribbing of studio engineer Mars, evolving into a debate about the hosts' actual feelings towards him. This led to a bizarre game attempting to coax Mars into saying "Chick-fil-A."  The conversation then shifted to a discussion of push-up form and the subjective nature of "pass" or "fail."

A substantial part of the show focused on a mysterious Opie and Anthony staff meeting.  Mars recounted a coworker, Roland's, alleged account of the meeting's directives regarding on-air versus off-air conduct, particularly the acceptable use of offensive language.  Roland supposedly cited an example of permissibly calling Mars a "fag" on-air while maintaining respectful off-air interactions.  Roland was subsequently called on-air, partially refuting Mars's version of events.  The conversation briefly touched on other topics, including "Sons of Anarchy," Anthony Weiner's "Wienergate" scandal, and a "Inspirational Jimmy" segment featuring ironically uninspiring tweets.  A listener called in, requesting bagel and bakery recommendations from Roland.

Later in the show, Club Soda Kenny, who had also attended the meeting, joined the conversation.  The broadcast also mentioned a news story about Memorial Day incidents and a previous report about a "leprechaun" sighting.  The show concluded with promotional plugs for Sam Roberts' "Poundstone Power Radio" and further humorous banter between the hosts and Club Soda Kenny, primarily focused on his Facebook page and lingering details about the Opie and Anthony staff meeting.
