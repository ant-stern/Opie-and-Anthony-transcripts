SHOW SUMMARY:

The Opie and Anthony Show broadcast opened with a discussion about the sabotage of their WNEW van, whose tire had come off in Manhattan, leaving employees Stinky and Pete stranded.  The hosts speculated about possible culprits, ranging from rivals to disgruntled individuals, fueled by the fact that all eight lug nuts were missing.  Numerous listener calls offered various theories, some attributing the incident to incompetence, others to malicious intent, and one caller even claimed to have seen Pete tampering with the tire earlier that day.  The discussion included anecdotes of similar tire incidents, including near-fatal accidents.  The hosts also heavily promoted their upcoming road show, mentioning guests such as Stephen Lynch, Fuel, and Q, and detailing plans for an air show by Paul the Pilot.  A caller jokingly confessed to the van sabotage, followed by a prank call from a fake Rob Halford.  The segment concluded with a Sobe beverage sponsorship plug and further road show details, including the possibility of attending a sticker stop event and the search for a replacement van, with a listener offering their own car.

The show continued with a call from Louis Black, vacationing in Puerto Rico, complaining about his resort and voicing disappointment with both Hillary Clinton and Rudy Giuliani in the New York Senate race. They jokingly proposed running a dog for office.  The conversation shifted back to the road show, confirming Jeff Norris, Eddie Otto, Big Billy Hayes, and Stephen Lynch as guests.
