SHOW SUMMARY:

The Opie and Anthony Show opened with a caller claiming to have slept with a vast number of New York City radio personalities, estimating around 75% of FM DJs and half of C100's staff.  The hosts then launched a contest for Pearl Jam concert tickets, the prize to be awarded to the listener who brought the most unusual guest or item to the studio. Initial options included livestock – a pig, goat, cow, or chicken – or a celebrity.

A caller then suggested a truly bizarre alternative: a naked woman and eggs, with the intent of attempting to incubate the eggs in the woman's breasts.  While initially incredulous, the hosts agreed to consider this entry if the caller could make it to the studio in time.

The show then shifted to a comedic segment featuring Anthony's surprisingly accurate Ronald Reagan impression.  This led to the hosts successfully obtaining George H.W. Bush's personal number in Kennebunkport, Maine, and subsequently attempting a prank call impersonating Reagan, leading to a humorous (though unspecified) exchange.

The Pearl Jam ticket contest resumed.  A listener ultimately won the tickets by bringing in a baby duckling.  Anthony's unexpectedly gentle interaction with the duckling became a recurring joke, especially after a friend revealed Anthony had eaten a mandarin duck over the weekend, creating a humorous contrast.  The show concluded with further discussion of the Reagan impersonation, the unorthodox Pearl Jam ticket contest entries, and more general banter between Opie and Anthony.
