The final segment featured Oswalt as a guest, continuing with off-color jokes and discussions about the Webby Awards,  the Michael Jackson hologram (comparing it to Tupac's hologram), and other explicit topics, including a heated and offensive exchange between caller Big D from Kentucky and Sal. The post-show with Sam Roberts followed, praising Oswalt.  The conversation circled back to the Billboard Music Awards and the Michael Jackson hologram, interspersed with more advertisements.  Numerous explicit sexual references and racial slurs were made by callers throughout the show.
