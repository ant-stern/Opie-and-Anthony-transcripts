SHOW SUMMARY:

The Opie and Anthony Show began with a discussion of a woman capable of projectile vomiting, followed by a quick greeting from Louis Black of *The Daily Show*.  The hosts then played Metallica and Creed, prompting listener complaints about their musical selections.  They shifted to celebrating the success of their unofficial website, approaching one million hits, featuring user-submitted photos (requiring O&A-related verification) and a video showcasing a past awkward crocodile encounter.  A “Girls of the Opie and Anthony Show” segment, using listener-submitted photos, was also highlighted. Rick's upcoming London trip and the return of a segment featuring a previously featured naked woman were mentioned.

The show moved to listener calls.  One caller detailed his snowy driving experiences, prompting a discussion of Opie's past car problems. Another caller humorously blamed Opie for his flu, leading to a conversation about their relative immunity to illness. Opie attributed his resistance to constant germ exposure from Anthony and the morning show host. Opie bragged about his behind-the-scenes efforts promoting the show in Boston, including his successful schmoozing with WBCN management and the resulting benefits, much to Anthony's irritation.  A further call recounted a terrifying engine failure at high speed. The segment concluded with the hosts repeatedly shouting "I'm going to take it."
