SHOW SUMMARY:

The Opie and Anthony Show broadcast opened with the hosts, Opie and Anthony, angrily complaining about their Sirius XM studio's deplorable conditions:  extremely poor lighting and inadequate heating.  Their frustration manifested in copious profanity and insults directed at building management and Sirius XM staff.  A space heater, employed to combat the cold, blew a circuit, plunging the studio into darkness and disabling their clocks. This malfunction sparked a listener conspiracy theory about manipulated broadcast times.  Numerous listener calls followed, with callers offering both support and criticism, engaging in banter and jokes with the hosts.  The conversation shifted to their exclusion from a "Top 100 Talkers" list, triggering a debate about their show's popularity and success.  They also lamented the initial plan to move their show to a premium XM channel, a decision they successfully opposed.  A substantial portion of the broadcast focused on a teacher's sex change and the resulting controversy and listener reactions, with the hosts offering their commentary and reactions to listener calls on the subject.   Further listener complaints arose, this time concerning one host's on-air flatulence.  The hosts also joked about a photoshopped Paltalk image of one of them. The discussion returned to the malfunctioning studio clocks. The show included a humorous parody song submitted by a caller, praised by the hosts, and a song featuring the repeated phrase "put the fucking lotion in the basket" along with graphic descriptions of violence and sexual assault.  The broadcast concluded with a discussion of a fan website and the importance of managing both positive and negative online feedback.  No guests appeared in the studio during the broadcast.
