SHOW SUMMARY:

The Opie and Anthony After Show, hosted by Sam Roberts, opened with a discussion surrounding the sixth anniversary of Terri Schiavo's death.  The segment featured audio of Schiavo's father emotionally, and at times profanely, attempting to communicate with her, alongside her own seemingly responsive, though unintelligible, vocalizations.  The hosts and crew debated the authenticity of the audio and the ethical considerations surrounding the Schiavo case.

Following this, the show moved on to recounting Opie and Anthony's appearance at an Imus event at the Hard Rock Cafe.  Audio from the event included an interview with Rob Bartlett, who humorously described his relationship with Imus as a mutually abusive one.  Post-event audio featuring Opie and Anthony's own reactions and observations on the event and audience response was played.  This segment also included an anecdote about crew member Eric Nagel's exaggerated claims of a close relationship with Sean Hannity.

A significant portion of the broadcast was dedicated to a comedic deconstruction of the movie *Twister*.  The hosts and callers dissected the film, mocking its plot, acting, and dialogue, pointing out perceived inaccuracies, particularly regarding locations.  Numerous audio clips from the movie were played to highlight the over-the-top nature of the acting and dialogue.

The show then shifted gears to celebrate Eddie Money's birthday.  This segment featured a previously recorded studio session where D-Boy, a crew member and avowed Eddie Money fan, sang "Two Tickets to Paradise" with Eddie Money himself.  D-Boy's performance and interactions with Eddie Money were discussed.  The birthday segment briefly touched upon the upcoming Ron and Fez show, its competition with Jim Norton's show on the Boneyard, and the hosts' experiences at a Donkey Kong competition.  Ron Bennington, the future host of the Ron and Fez show, briefly joined the conversation near the end.  The segment also mentioned a planned appearance by a tornado hunter, who ultimately did not appear on the show.
