The hosts also harshly criticized the New York Mets' performance, particularly Mike Piazza's, following their playoff elimination.  This transitioned into a recurring theme exploring the disparity between the hosts' envisioned ideal weekend (involving drinking and socializing) and their actual weekends, largely dictated by their partners (pumpkin picking, apple cider, etc.). The broadcast concluded with a brief, abrupt discussion about the hosts' contrasting views on relationships versus single life. No studio guests were mentioned in this portion.

While praising the episode overall and highlighting Cameron Diaz's appearance (and apparent lack of a bra), the hosts vehemently criticized a sketch featuring Dan Aykroyd and Steve Martin as the "Wild and Crazy Guys," deeming their performance subpar and declaring them past their prime.
