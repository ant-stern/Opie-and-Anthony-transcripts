SHOW SUMMARY:

The Opie and Anthony Show broadcast began with a bit where the hosts jokingly declared themselves an anti-terrorist squad, encouraging listeners to report suspicious activity via phone or fax.  This quickly segued into a sexually explicit discussion of the Monica Lewinsky scandal, fueled by details from the Drudge Report, focusing on graphic descriptions of alleged sexual acts between Lewinsky and President Clinton.  Listener calls followed, reporting increasingly absurd "suspicious" activities, ranging from a man holding a pro-impeachment and anti-Afghanistan sign to a man who could dance exceptionally well and a racially charged basketball game.  The Lewinsky discussion continued, interwoven with a contest offering a prize to the first woman to flash her breasts on the Today Show. Anthony's wife, Jen, briefly joined the show, offering a contrasting perspective on infidelity and the Clinton scandal.

The conversation then shifted to Hurricane Bonnie, with the hosts mocking weather reports and suggesting more dramatic hurricane names.  They discussed Clinton's impending resignation/impeachment,  and a listener contest to guess the date.  The hosts then launched into a discussion on the poor taste of merchandise related to tragedies, citing the Titanic and Flight 800, and even venturing into a hypothetical discussion about Holocaust merchandise.  Further news items were covered, including a woman sending cyanide in the mail, Mark McGwire and Sammy Sosa's home run race, and pregnancies amongst the Spice Girls.  The segment concluded with a lengthy, graphic, and sexually suggestive discussion about conjoined twins, specifically a Dateline segment featuring a particular pair, and assigned listeners the "homework" of watching the segment and discussing it on the next show.

The following segment started with a nonsensical mathematical joke involving the number 69 and oral sex.  The hosts then discussed their reactions to seeing unusual people, such as a two-headed person, comparing it to witnessing car accidents. Opie recounted an encounter with a Middle Eastern couple showcasing differing cultural attire.  The main focus then returned to the $700 offer for a woman to flash her breasts on the Today Show. Detailed instructions on how to execute this "terrorist boobing" were provided, referencing a perceived rivalry with the Today Show stemming from a previous Chris Rock appearance.  The hosts stressed that only the first successful flasher would receive the money. The show ended with the hosts stating their intention to watch the Dateline segment on Siamese twins, and a humorous aside speculating about Tom Brokaw reporting on a cigar-related rumor concerning Monica Lewinsky.
