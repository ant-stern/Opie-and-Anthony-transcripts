The Opie and Anthony Show broadcast began with a humorous recounting of a false fire alarm in their building, focusing on their building's fire warden, Earl, whom they accused of abandoning his post and fleeing before them. The hosts then discussed graphic videos of Taliban executions from a website, transitioning into a segment heavily featuring listener calls filled with highly offensive language expressing anger at Hillary Clinton's perceived disinterest during a presidential address.

This argument escalated with calls from Mark’s brother and other listeners, involving accusations of incompetence and personal attacks. The segment concluded with more discussion about the false fire alarm and Earl’s perceived failings.  Throughout this section, numerous callers contributed to the chaotic and offensive atmosphere.
