SHOW SUMMARY:

The Opie and Anthony Show opened with comedian Joe Piscopo as a guest.  Piscopo, claiming to be lost on his way to New Jersey,  entered the studio and reacted to the show's already-in-progress content, commenting on segments involving breast implants and a guest known only as "Buddy."  A significant portion of the show then centered on Vixen, a completely nude, petite woman from New Jersey, who was present in the studio.  The hosts and Piscopo engaged in discussions about Vixen's physique, her origins, and her unusual meeting with her husband at Chuck E. Cheese.  Vixen's nudity was a central feature, with conversations revolving around her participation in activities like nude hopscotch.  The hosts also joked about the possibility of putting Vixen on a local all-news station.  Later in the broadcast, the tone shifted considerably as Piscopo discussed his charitable work with at-risk children through his Positive Impact Foundation and an upcoming NBC television show.  This contrasted sharply with the earlier, raunchier segments featuring Vixen.  The show also included unidentified listeners who were given access to the studio at some point.
