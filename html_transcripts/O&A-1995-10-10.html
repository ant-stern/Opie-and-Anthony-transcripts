SHOW SUMMARY:

The Opie and Anthony Show opened with a listener's quirky question about mailing an unpackaged banana, which a post office employee confirmed was permissible.  Following this, the hosts welcomed in-studio guests Robbie and John from the Goo Goo Dolls.  The band's initial audio suffered from a significant reverb effect, leading to jokes comparing the sound quality to papal broadcasts from the Popemobile.  Conversation turned to the band's demanding summer tour, revealing they'd only had fleeting visits back to their hometown of Buffalo.  This prompted a darkly humorous tangent about reckless and often fatal stunts at Niagara Falls, including a failed jet ski attempt, a suicide by kayak, and a harrowing drug-related incident near the falls' edge. The segment concluded with the Goo Goo Dolls performing a song, offering a preview of a forthcoming single they characterized as a departure from their current chart-topping hit.
