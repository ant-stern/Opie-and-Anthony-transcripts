SHOW SUMMARY:

The Opie and Anthony Show broadcast featured a central stunt: the "55-gallon drum challenge."  Five women – Christy, Wendy, Kate, Jane, and Tabitha – were recruited to participate.  Before the challenge began, the hosts, Opie and Anthony, along with several studio assistants including "Psycho Mark" and Rick, prepared five 55-gallon drums (four standard, one oversized).  The women were interviewed and their attire described in detail before being placed inside the sealed drums.  Listener Pete Marchados donated $1000, and the women received a $1000 Shrewsbury Bicycles gift certificate.  A caller, Ken, offered placenta, which was declined.  Listeners were encouraged to contribute items, resulting in the acquisition of three five-gallon buckets of "chum" and other materials.  Air holes were drilled into the drums, and the initial substances poured in were beer and water.

The challenge itself involved pouring a progressively disgusting array of substances into the barrels.  This included beer, water, Sobe, cat litter, raw eggs, corn oil, lotion, V8 juice, Worcestershire sauce, Aunt Jemima syrup, a large quantity of live and dead fish (including a squid, sardines, and a larger fish with its guts and eyeballs), and "clam chum" (a mixture of ground corn, fish guts, and other unspecified ingredients).  Christy's barrel broke, spilling its contents.  The increasingly foul smell caused studio crew member Rick to become violently ill.  Andrew Dice Clay made a brief call.  The show aired commercials for Paisano's restaurant, the Razor Rollerboard Scooter from Sharper Image, and Mouchon hair removal wax.  A New Rochelle Toyota dealership representative called to describe the crowd's reaction to the broadcast at their location. Callers offered suggestions and reactions, with one mentioning the potential for maggots.  Further substances added included chocolate syrup, dog food, anchovies, prune juice, sardines, and live mealworms or similar insects.

The challenge continued with crickets, wax worms, mealworms, and finally, large hissing cockroaches. Kate and Christy exited first due to discomfort, increasing the prize money for the remaining contestants – Wendy, Jane, and Tabitha – who ultimately shared the winnings.  Joey Paisano offered $1000 in gift certificates to his restaurant, split among the participants.  The women expressed varying levels of distress and disgust, with reactions ranging from screaming and visible discomfort to giggling and numb acceptance.  The hosts highlighted the sound bites and seemed to enjoy the women’s reactions.  Callers included Tyrell (thanking the show for a van at an event) and "Gorilla Boy" (sharing a humorous encounter with fans).  The show concluded with the women offering brief comments, plugs, and thanks to listeners.  Jeff Norris, a staff member who also participated, returned to the studio.  The show’s website, WNAW.com (or possibly dw.com), was promoted, featuring 170 pictures from the event.  The hosts ended with further humorous remarks.
