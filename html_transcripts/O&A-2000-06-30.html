SHOW SUMMARY:

The Opie and Anthony show opened with a segment titled "Mommy, What Would You Do For Britney Spears Tickets?"  The premise involved soliciting calls from mothers willing to perform outrageous stunts for tickets. While the hosts anticipated more extreme entries, only one mother, "Banana Rose," physically appeared in the studio.  Her initial offer – inserting a banana into her exhaust pipe – proved underwhelming, leading to considerable frustration and comedic bickering between Opie and Anthony.  Their disappointment fueled a rapid escalation of increasingly absurd stunt suggestions, culminating in a challenge involving Rose launching a bottle rocket from her vagina using a custom-made plexiglass shield.  Despite initial hesitation, Anthony ultimately agreed to provide Rose with Britney Spears tickets and a limo ride upon successful completion.  The show then detailed preparations for the stunt, mentioning safety measures like a fire extinguisher and a garbage pail.  The stunt was successfully executed, with the bottle rocket striking a cardboard cutout of El Duque.  The segment concluded with celebratory remarks from the hosts and the fulfillment of Rose's prize, solidifying her status as a memorable guest.
